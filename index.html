Honest answers

The questions every family asks first.

Isn't private home care expensive compared to a care home?

A private care-home room in Winnipeg often runs $3,000–$6,000+ per month — for everything, whether you need it or not. With TrueHeart you pay only for the hours you actually use, and funding streams like WRHA Self & Family Managed Care or VAC can offset part or all of the cost. Request your rate below and we'll book a free 15-minute discovery call to walk through your exact cost — most families are surprised how it compares.

What if we only need a few hours a week?

That's exactly how most families start. There's no minimum contract — a 3-hour respite visit on Saturdays counts. Care can scale up naturally as needs change.

Who exactly will be coming into my parent's home?

An experienced, vetted Health Care Aide working under a care plan built and reviewed by Mary, our co-founder and Registered Nurse — with a current criminal record check, adult abuse registry check, and WCB coverage. Because TrueHeart is founder-led, you'll know exactly who's coming, every visit. You meet them before care ever begins.

Can government funding pay for this?

Often, yes. We work with WRHA Self & Family Managed Care (you receive funding and choose your own provider), Veterans Affairs Canada benefits, and most long-term care insurance plans. On our first call, we'll tell you honestly which streams you may qualify for and help with the paperwork.

What can a Health Care Aide actually help with?

Personal care (bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting), safe transfers and mobility, meal preparation, medication reminders, light housekeeping and laundry, companionship, errands and appointment escorts — plus certain health-related tasks performed under direction. We'll map exactly what's needed during the free assessment.

What if it doesn't work out?

Your first visit is covered by our guarantee — not satisfied, you don't pay. After that, there's no long-term contract; you can pause or cancel with 24 hours' notice. We earn every visit.
